    Airedale Community Trading (ACT) Ltd, located at The Active Centre in Airedale, Castleford, operates as a dynamic social enterprise. Our mission combines environmental stewardship with community service, ensuring that our actions benefit both people and the planet.

    • By involving young people in our initiatives (such as computer recycling or car clinic operations), ACT fosters a sense of responsibility, work ethic, and community engagement.
